More Than a Hair Appointment.

Most salon experiences follow a rigid script: Consult. Service. Checkout. But hair holds identity. It reflects transition, stress, growth, confidence, and reinvention. When you walk into The Velvet Salon, you’re stepping into a space where the conversation matters just as much as the cut. We slow down here. We tune in. We create from the inside out.

20+ Years Behind the Chair | Deep Listening | Precision Craft

I’ve never fit into one aesthetic—I’ve explored them all. From goth to minimalist, from loud to understated, self-expression has always been part of how I move through the world. Over time, I learned that beauty doesn’t need to be loud to be powerful. My salon is calm, low-toxic, and sensory-aware—a private, one-on-one space where you can exhale.

Haircuts are my foundation. I specialize in precision shorts, razor and shear hybrid cutting (perfect for thick, heavy hair that needs internal movement without looking shredded), and tailoring curly cuts to your real life—not a rigid method. My approach is detailed, technical, and collaborative. I listen hard, study your inspiration, and refine until it feels spot on.

When it comes to color, my lane is effortless: soft gray blending, lived-in organic tones, and non-ammonia color that grows out beautifully. I believe in simple routines and clean, multifunctional haircare. Let’s be honest—we’ve all bought flashy products we didn’t need and didn't feel better after. I’m tired of that. If it’s on my shelf, it earned its place. Less noise. More intention. Better hair.

Clarity Conversations | Meaningful Perspective

Not every conversation fits perfectly inside a typical salon appointment. Sometimes what people are really looking for is clarity. Hair can often open the door, but when you want to go deeper into life transitions, decisions, or personal exploration, that is where my work naturally complements this space.

I offer clarity conversations for people navigating change and personal growth. My approach isn’t about teaching a system, giving you the "right" answers, or fitting you into a rigid framework. Instead, I create a space for honest conversation and thoughtful reflection.

With a calm and grounded presence, I help clients slow down, untangle what feels unclear, see patterns more clearly, and reconnect with their own inner knowing. Through these conversations, insight is allowed to emerge naturally, without pressure or expectation. Everything here is invitational.
